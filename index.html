Support and Privacy Policy

About RunPace

RunPace is a simple race pace calculator designed to help runners calculate goal pace and estimate same-pace finish times for common race distances.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

RunPace does not collect, store, track, sell, or share any personal data.

The app does not require an account, login, email address, name, location, health data, contacts, photos, or payment information.

All calculations happen locally on your device.

Analytics and Tracking

RunPace does not use analytics tools, advertising networks, tracking technologies, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Internet Access

RunPace does not require internet access to calculate pace or finish time estimates.

Children’s Privacy

RunPace does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if RunPace changes in the future. Any updates will be posted on this page.
